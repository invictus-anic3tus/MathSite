Geometry and Shapes

Rectangle Area: \( A = \text{length} \times \text{width} \)

Square Area: \( A = \text{side}^2 \)

Triangle Area: \( A = \frac{1}{2} \times \text{base} \times \text{height} \)

Circle Area: \( A = \pi r^2 \)

Trapezoid Area: \( A = \frac{1}{2} \times (\text{base}_1 + \text{base}_2) \times \text{height} \)

Parallelogram Area: \( A = \text{base} \times \text{height} \)

Perimeter of a Rectangle: \( P = 2 \times (\text{length} + \text{width}) \)

Perimeter of a Square: \( P = 4 \times \text{side} \)

Circumference of a Circle: \( C = 2 \pi r \)

Volumes

Cube: \( V = \text{side}^3 \)

Rectangular Prism: \( V = \text{length} \times \text{width} \times \text{height} \)

Cylinder: \( V = \pi r^2 h \)

Sphere: \( V = \frac{4}{3} \pi r^3 \)

Cone: \( V = \frac{1}{3} \pi r^2 h \)

Pyramid: \( V = \frac{1}{3} \times \text{base area} \times \text{height} \)

Graphs & Lines

Slope of a Line: \( m = \frac{y_2 - y_1}{x_2 - x_1} \)

Equation of a Line (Slope-Intercept Form): \( y = mx + b \)

Point-Slope Form: \( y - y_1 = m(x - x_1) \)

Distance Between Two Points: \( d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} \)

Midpoint Formula: \( M = \left( \frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2} \right) \)

Quadratics

Quadratic Formula: \( x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a} \)

Vertex Form: \( y = a(x - h)^2 + k \)

Axis of Symmetry: \( x = \frac{-b}{2a} \)

Discriminant: \( \Delta = b^2 - 4ac \)

Trigonometry

Sine: \( \sin \theta = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{hypotenuse}} \)

Cosine: \( \cos \theta = \frac{\text{adjacent}}{\text{hypotenuse}} \)

Tangent: \( \tan \theta = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}} \)

Pythagorean Identity: \( \sin^2 \theta + \cos^2 \theta = 1 \)

Law of Sines: \( \frac{\sin A}{a} = \frac{\sin B}{b} = \frac{\sin C}{c} \)

Law of Cosines: \( c^2 = a^2 + b^2 - 2ab \cos C \)

Statistics

Mean: \( \text{Mean} = \frac{\text{Sum of all data points}}{\text{Number of data points}} \)

Median: The middle value in a data set when ordered from smallest to largest.

Mode: The most frequently occurring value(s) in a data set.

Variance: \( \sigma^2 = \frac{\sum (x_i - \mu)^2}{N} \)

Standard Deviation: \( \sigma = \sqrt{\frac{\sum (x_i - \mu)^2}{N}} \)

Probability: \( P(E) = \frac{\text{Number of favorable outcomes}}{\text{Total number of outcomes}} \)

Sequences & Series

Arithmetic Sequence (nth term): \( a_n = a_1 + (n - 1) \cdot d \)

Arithmetic Series (Sum): \( S_n = \frac{n}{2} \cdot (a_1 + a_n) \)

Geometric Sequence (nth term): \( a_n = a_1 \cdot r^{n-1} \)

Geometric Series (Sum): \( S_n = a_1 \cdot \frac{1 - r^n}{1 - r} \) (for \( r \neq 1 \))

Infinite Geometric Series: \( S = \frac{a_1}{1 - r} \) (for \( |r| < 1 \))
